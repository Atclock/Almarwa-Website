Industrial Fuel:

- To ensure a healthy environment and longer lifespan for factory burners with increased performance.

- We manufacture industrial fuel according to the best Jordanian specifications and standards, which are designated as (JS2277:2021). Moreover, Al-Marwa company for manufacturing and refining mineral oils holds a license for industrial fuel production from the Jordanian Energy and Minerals Regulatory Commision.

Why us?

- The viscosity ratio is suitable for extending the lifespan of burner parts.

- High Calorific value helps to save fuel consumption.

- Appropriate flash point for operation within various temperatures and weather conditions.

- The sulfur content is within or lower than the globally and environmentally permissible levels

- Completely moisture-free (0.00%), enhancing combustion efficiency and protecting fuel system components.

- Each order undergoes diesel quality testing to ensure reliability.

Delivery Service
